About the Team

The Knights 11U are a Bay Area travel baseball team competing at the highest youth level. Our players train hard, play with heart, and represent their families and communities with pride — on and off the field.

We are currently preparing for one of youth baseball’s most iconic experiences: a trip to Cooperstown Dreams Park in Summer 2027. This week-long tournament in Cooperstown, New York draws over 100 teams from across the country and is a once-in-a-lifetime milestone for every player.

The Cooperstown Dream

Cooperstown Dreams Park in Cooperstown, NY hosts one of the most prestigious youth baseball tournaments in the world. We’re raising funds so every Knight can make the trip in Summer 2027 — no player left behind.
